    Grand Event Hall

    About Us

    Where Dreams Meet Reality

    Nityotsava, a distinguished venue for celebratory excellence, is located in the heart of Mysore near Ballal Circle and Sharada Vilas College. Inaugurated on March 22, 2015, by the esteemed poet Prof. Dr. Nizar Ahmed, Nityotsava has epitomized seamless celebratory experiences, allowing clients to fully enjoy their moments while creating cherished memories. As a sister concern of Nithyananda Associates Pvt Ltd, Nityotsava embraces a verdant theme with natural ventilation, ample sunlight, Mangalore tile roofing, and a commitment to rainwater harvesting. Operating as a zero-plastic zone, Nityotsava replaces plastic with eco-conscious alternatives such as polycarbonate cups for mineral water and Matka cups or durable steel ware for ice creams and desserts.
