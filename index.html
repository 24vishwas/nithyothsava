    Grand Event Hall

    Your events need to be picture perfect – our spacious facility seats up to a 1000 of your guests. Our grand hall is a fully air-conditioned venue ideal for performances and VIP visits. Designed to match your style and suit your decorations, with enough space so people in the back don’t miss your memorable moments.

    Expansive hall

    A cosier hall for the more private moments in life. This hall has an expansive layout that seats 200 people - ideal for smaller gatherings and business parties. It may be reserved in conjunction with the grand hall, which doubles as a great venue for food and other events that don't need a large stage. The smaller space also allows for the setup of an air cooler, for those that find the A/C too cold.

    About Us

    Grandeur, Greenery, Glamour & Grace. A Hall That Does It All!

    Nityotsava is a top venue for all your celebrations, located in the heart of Mysore near Ballal Circle and Sharada Vilas College. Nityotsava Hall was inaugurated on March 22, 2015, by the esteemed poet Prof. Dr. Nizar Ahmed.

    Sustainability and Amenities

    At Nityotsava, we respect nature. Our venue features natural ventilation, plenty of sunlight, and traditional Mangalore tile roofing. We're a zero-plastic zone, using eco-friendly options like polycarbonate cups and steel ware. Plus, we responsibly manage waste, with surplus food helping local orphanages and community piggeries.     Hygienic

    Before use, market-sourced vegetables are thoroughly sterilized in a sunlit, well-ventilated area to ensure optimal cleanliness. Our storeroom is regularly maintained with effective pest control methods to uphold hygienic conditions. Additionally, we are GMP and GHP certified with an FSSAI license, reflecting our steadfast commitment to hygiene.

    Grand Ambiance

    With our dedication to the greatest quality and hygienic standards, we guarantee the finest experience for our visitors. Our Ambiance offers the best of both worlds to give you with the best venue ideal for all your gatherings. In addition, we maintain a plastic-free zone, clean, well-maintained rooms with a green theme, clean drinking water, and a RO plant to guarantee our visitors have the best possible stay.

    Plastic free zone

    We uphold a 100% plastic-free environment, providing water from an RO plant served in polycarbonate cups, and using mud pots for cooling. Our second hall utilizes natural cooling without air conditioning. The building is a green structure, featuring rainwater harvesting, solar energy utilization, and a minimal carbon footprint.
