    Grand Event Hall

    Your events need to be picture perfect – our spacious facility seats up to a 1000 of your guests. Our grand hall is a fully air-conditioned venue ideal for performances and VIP visits.     About Us

    Grandeur, Greenery, Glamour & Grace. A Hall That Does It All!

    Nityotsava is a top venue for all your celebrations, located in the heart of Mysore near Ballal Circle and Sharada Vilas College. Nityotsava Hall was inaugurated on March 22, 2015, by the esteemed poet Prof. Dr. Nizar Ahmed.

    Sustainability and Amenities

    At Nityotsava, we respect nature. Our venue features natural ventilation, plenty of sunlight, and traditional Mangalore tile roofing. We're a zero-plastic zone, using eco-friendly options like polycarbonate cups and steel ware. Plus, we responsibly manage waste, with surplus food helping local orphanages and community piggeries.     Top-Class Hygiene

    We prioritise maintaining a clean and safe environment for all our guests.

    • Sterilised Vegetables: Before use, we thoroughly clean market-sourced vegetables in a sunlit, well-ventilated area.
    • Pest Control: Our storeroom is regularly maintained with effective pest control methods.
    • Certified Standards: We hold GMP and GHP certifications and are licensed by FSSAI.

    Ambiance That Wows!

    We dedicate ourselves to providing the highest quality and hygienic standards. A few hours here, but a lifetime of memories at our venue!

    • Quality: We prioritise cleanliness and quality to create a pleasant and safe environment.
    • Well-Maintained Rooms: Our rooms are clean, well-kept, and designed with a green theme.
    • Clean Drinking Water: We provide clean drinking water through an RO plant to ensure your health and comfort.

    Earth-Friendly

    Every celebration is memorable and sustainable at Nityotsava.

    • Plastic-Free Zone: We are committed to maintaining a 100% plastic-free environment.
    • Natural Cooling: Our second hall uses natural cooling methods without air conditioning.
    • Low Carbon Footprint: Our venue boasts eco-friendly features like rainwater harvesting and solar energy use.
