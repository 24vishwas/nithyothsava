    Grand Event Hall

    Our expansive venue accommodates 800 to 1,000 guests, featuring elegant chair covers and full air conditioning for ultimate comfort. Adjacent to the stage are two green rooms, perfect for performers or VIP guests.

    Expansive hall

    This hall offers a versatile space, accommodating 200 delegates making it ideal for corporate functions and mini events where this can be booked with combination to the grand hall serving as an ideal location for food and other secondary events which does not require a massive stage. This also provides a non Ac Air cooler option.

    About Us

    Where Dreams Meet Reality

    Nityotsava, a premier venue in Mysore near Ballal Circle and Sharada Vilas College, has epitomized celebratory excellence since its inauguration by Prof. Dr. Nizar Ahmed on March 22nd, 2015. Clients trust Nityotsava to create seamless, memorable experiences in an eco-friendly, zero-plastic environment that respects nature and prioritizes sustainability.

    Hygienic

    We follow the hygiene standards very strictly where we try to ensure the highest standards of cleanliness with best hygiene practices.

    Grand Ambiance

    We provide the best ambience for every event with space that is suitable for all sorts of events and blends with every design language. With a great garden area and location is a plus being in the center of the city.

    Plastic free zone

    Our whole hall is a plastic free zone. We try our best to minimize the usage of plastic by serving water with water glass instead of bottle. We try to be as eco friendly as possible and we encourage our guests to follow the same.
