    Catering Services

    Culinary Excellence

    At Nityotsava, culinary excellence is at the heart of our ethos. Our kitchen is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and top-of-the-line stainless steel appliances, ensuring optimal preparation for every meal. Led by an expert chef and a team of highly experienced cooks, we blend passion and skill to create dishes that are not only delicious but also visually stunning.

    Unwavering Hygiene Standards

    Hygiene is paramount in everything we do. Our high-quality stainless steel cutlery sets are meticulously maintained, and every utensil is thoroughly sanitized after each use by our dedicated cleaning staff. We ensure that cleanliness and hygiene are upheld at all times to provide a safe and enjoyable dining experience.

    Itinerary: A Culinary Experience Made with Love

    Our exclusive vegetarian menu is crafted with care, focusing on hygiene, quality, and natural ingredients. We avoid artificial colors, flavors, and preservatives to deliver a culinary experience that delights the senses. As a testament to our commitment, we are GMP & GHP certified with a valid FSSAI number, setting the benchmark for exceptional vegetarian cuisine.

    Sterilization and Storage

    Before use, vegetables undergo thorough sterilization in a sunlit, well-ventilated area to maintain optimal cleanliness. Our store room area receives regular attention with effective pest control measures to uphold hygienic conditions. Food items are stored in tightly sealed containers to preserve freshness, sourced only from first-grade raw materials.
