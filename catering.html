    Amazing Food

    At Nityotsava, we strive to keep our meals delicious and your mouths watering! Our kitchen is packed with state-of-the-art technology and top-of-the-line stainless steel appliances, ready to whip up delicious dishes. Our expert chef and talented team of cooks put their hearts into every meal, making sure everything looks as good as it tastes.

    Clean and Hygienic

    Cleanliness is a big deal for us. We use top-quality stainless steel cutlery, and our dedicated cleaning team makes sure everything is spotless after each use.

    Food Made with Love

    Our special vegetarian menu focuses on using all natural ingredients. We keep away from artificial colours, flavours, and preservatives. We’re proud to be GMP, GHP and FSSAI certified.

    Fresh and Clean

    Before we use any vegetables, we make sure they’re thoroughly cleaned in a sunny, well-ventilated area. Our storage area gets regular pest control treatments to keep it hygienic. We store food in tightly sealed containers to keep it fresh, using only the best quality ingredients.
