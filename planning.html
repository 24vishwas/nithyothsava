    Event Planning Assistance

    Focus On Enjoyment - Leave The Planning To Us!

    Planning an event can be stressful, but that's where we come in! Our top-notch event planning services cover everything you need for a smooth and memorable occasion. We coordinate decor, catering, and logistics. Our experienced team takes care of it all with precision and care.

    Tailored to Your Taste

    Whether it's a corporate event, a wedding, or a special celebration, we customise our services to fit your specific needs and preferences. Our goal is to create an unforgettable experience for you and your guests, letting you relax and enjoy the moment without any worries.
    With our dedicated approach and keen attention to detail, we ensure that every part of your event is flawlessly executed. Nityotsava aims to exceed your expectations with our professional event planning expertise.

    Extra Special, Extra Touches To Light Up Your Nights

    We offer a variety of extras to make your event truly special. We offer stunning decorations to professional photography and videography. Purohith or an event anchor are also available on request.
    If you want to add a touch of tradition to your event, we also organise Naadaswara instrument players (volaga) to fill the air with beautiful, melodic tunes.

    Light up your event with our thoughtfully designed hall and podium lighting and create a captivating atmosphere for your guests.

    Sound and Light, Done Just Right With Tech Support

    We provide top-notch technical support to keep the lighting, sound, and music at your event running smoothly. Our team of technical experts is dedicated to setting the perfect ambiance for corporate functions, weddings, or even private celebrations.

    Sound and Light

    We set up advanced lighting systems, manage audio equipment and ensure flawless music playback. Enjoy your event without worrying, while we work behind the scenes.
