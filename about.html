    Beautiful Memories & Grand Celebrations!

    Our Story

    Welcome to Nityotsava,
    Nityotsava is a top venue for all your celebrations, located in the heart of Mysore near Ballal Circle and Sharada Vilas College. Nityotsava Hall was inaugurated on March 22, 2015, by the esteemed poet Prof. Dr. Nizar Ahmed.

    We strive to be an eco-friendly destination that frames your memories so every moment you spend with us is unforgettable. Nityotsava Hall is designed with natural ventilation, ample sunlight, and Mangalore tile roofing. We also practise rainwater harvesting to support sustainability.

    We are proud to be a zero-plastic zone. We use eco-friendly alternatives like polycarbonate cups for mineral water and Matka cups or durable steel ware for ice creams and desserts.

    Hall Features and Services

    Grand Event Hall

    Your events need to be picture perfect – our spacious facility seats up to a 1000 of your guests. Our grand hall is a fully air-conditioned venue ideal for performances and VIP visits. Designed to match your style and suit your decorations, with enough space so people in the back don’t miss your memorable moments.

    Expansive hall

    A cosier hall for the more private moments in life. This hall has an expansive layout that seats 200 people - ideal for smaller gatherings and business parties. It may be reserved in conjunction with the grand hall, which doubles as a great venue for food and other events that don't need a large stage.

    Clean & Green With Excellent Cuisine!

    Our kitchen has the latest tech and a team of skilled chefs. We keep things spotless and clean with high-quality stainless steel cutlery and strict cleaning practices. Our vegetarian menu is made with natural ingredients and no artificial additives. Every meal is made with care, to ensure the best dining that Mysore has to offer. Fresh flavours, tidy kitchen - Happy you!

    Security To Comfort - We Take Care of Everything

    Nityotsava Hall offers more than just an excellent venue. Security with comprehensive CCTV surveillance and a team of trained guards ensuring your safety. No more parking worries, all your arrivals will remain smooth and stress-free! We provide ample space for cars and two-wheelers.
    We also provide event planning services, including decorations, photography, and videography, so you can have a perfect event without any hassle.

    The comfort of our guests is top priority. We offer dormitory accommodations with air-conditioned rooms and modern amenities.

    Making Your Moments Magical - Exp

    Nityotsava Hall, Mysore offers safety, style, comfort, and celebration. Our aim is to make your ‘event’ become ‘event of the season’ - from weddings and birthdays to corporate meetings and social gatherings. Every moment becomes a cherished memory. Join us and make your celebration amazing.
