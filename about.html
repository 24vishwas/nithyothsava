    About

    Our Story

    Welcome to Nityotsava,
    Located in the heart of Mysore near the iconic Ballal Circle and revered Sharada Vilas College, Nityotsava is synonymous with celebratory excellence. Established on March 22nd, 2015, by the esteemed 'Nityotsava' poet, Prof. Dr. Nizar Ahmed, our venue has since curated seamless celebratory experiences, where clients entrust us with their festivities, crafting cherished memories for every gathering.

    Sustainability and Amenities,
    Respecting nature, Nityotsava embraces a verdant theme with natural ventilation, ample sunlight, and timeless Mangalore tile roofing. We are committed to sustainability by operating as a zero-plastic zone, using eco-conscious alternatives like polycarbonate cups and durable steel ware. Our dedication extends to responsible waste management, where surplus food nourishes local orphanages and supports community piggeries.

    Hall Features and Services

    Grand Event Hall

    With exquisite chair coverings and complete air conditioning for maximum comfort, our spacious facility can host up to 1,000 people. There are two green rooms next to the stage, which are ideal for performers or VIP visitors. The hall is also the ideal location for formal events like weddings since it blends in perfectly with any decoration and allows every guest to have the greatest view of the whole stage.

    Expansive hall

    With its flexible layout that can hold up to 200 guests, this hall is perfect for small gatherings and business parties. It may be reserved in conjunction with the grand hall, which doubles as a great venue for food and other events that don't need a large stage. This offers an alternative to an air cooler without AC.

    Culinary Excellence and Hygiene Standards

    Our kitchen embodies culinary excellence with state-of-the-art technology and a passionate team led by expert chefs. We prioritize hygiene with high-quality stainless steel cutlery and meticulous sanitation practices. Our vegetarian menu, crafted with natural ingredients and devoid of artificial additives, sets a benchmark in quality and taste.

    Additional Facilities

    Additional amenities include comprehensive CCTV surveillance, trained security personnel, and ample parking for cars and two-wheelers. We also offer event planning services, decorations, photography, videography, and more to ensure a seamless event experience. Our commitment to comfort extends to dormitory accommodations with air-conditioned rooms and modern facilities.

    Experience Nityotsava

    Whether you're planning a wedding, corporate event, or special celebration, Nityotsava offers a blend of elegance, functionality, and sustainability. Our venue is designed to exceed expectations, providing a memorable setting for every occasion.
